VISUALIZED CSDP PROJECT IN EDO STATE AS AT 2013

About

Edo State Community and Social Development Program Map is a visualization of completed micro-projects by CSDP in the various Local Government Areas in the state as at August,2013.

These visualizations utilize data collected in 2013 and published on the Edo State Open Data Portal.

This application was developed by the Open Data Team of Information Communication Technology Agency, Edo state. Copyright (c), 2013.
